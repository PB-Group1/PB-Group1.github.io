The primary goal of the Aphasia App is to improve the speech production and day-to-day activities of people afflicted with aphasia.



The Aphasia App is a mobile app available on Android platforms built using dart and the Flutter SDK. It utilizes deep learning through TensorFlow to calculate where the user's gaze is on the screen to automatically read words aloud using text-to-speech. The Aphasia App is bases itself on the idea of Melodic Intonation Therapy (MIT) by shifting the pitch of each syllable read to make the reading more songlike.
